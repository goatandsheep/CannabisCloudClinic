Healthcare Practitioners

Refer your patients to our medical cannabis specialists.

Cannabis has alleviated patient symptoms where conventional therapeutic options have failed. For physicians that are committed to maximizing patient comfort, medical cannabis should be considered as a natural treatment alternative. Here, in Canada, the Access to Cannabis for Medical Purposes Regulations (ACMPR) set out the guidelines for safely and properly prescribing cannabis for patients.

Cannabis Cloud Clinic is a partner in your patients’ health.

Preparing for consultation

If your patient is considering cannabis as a natural treatment alternative, please prepare the Relevant Health Information for their appropriate clinic, e.g.

  • Patient's pertinent consult / case assessment report(s)
  • Patient's complete list of prescribed medication(s)
  • Patient's complete list of non-prescribed drug(s), i.e over-the-counter medications (OTCs)
  • Patient's complete list of health / nutritional / dietary supplement(s) such as vitamins, minerals, herbs, meal supplements, sports nutrition products, natural food supplements, or other related product(s) that has been tried or considered for conventional / alternative treatment(s) and that have been found to be ineffective or medically inappropriate for the treatment of the corresponding medical condition
  • Information about any other form/s of therapy previously received or are presently receive to treat the eligible condition's symptom(s)
  • Patient's pertinent laboratory test(s), scans/imaging
