Healthcare Practitioners

Refer your patients to our medical cannabis specialists.

Cannabis has alleviated patient symptoms where conventional therapeutic options have failed. For physicians that are committed to maximizing patient comfort, medical cannabis should be considered as a natural treatment alternative. Here, in Canada, the Access to Cannabis for Medical Purposes Regulations (ACMPR) set out the guidelines for safely and properly prescribing cannabis for patients.

Cannabis Cloud Clinic is a partner in your patients’ health. We have a host of resources related to the prescription and use of cannabis for medical purposes that will help guide you through the process, such as the guidelines of the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Ontario. Our specialists will contact your patients remotely and securely via the cloud video tools convenient to them. Most importantly we want to ensure you’re equipped with the right information to make a well informed decision with your patient.

If you’re interested in obtaining more information about the entire process of prescribing cannabis to patients, simply contact us and a representative will be happy to connect with you.
